-->> Discussing the very important benefits of tea

There are many joys and benefits of making ourselves a simple cup of tea.

  • One health benefit is, it helps to 're-hydrate' both our internal/external bodily systems going all over.
  • Second, it seems to refresh us even down to our very own soul...making us approach life with much renewed vigour and determination.
  • Usually, people smile/and, feel so much warmer/livelier...when they've had a really good cup of tea.

-(DISCLAIMER: There are some people who would much prefer to drink coffee, instead;
but, myself I was always a born tea drinker...I find drinking coffee to be much too bitter to taste...?!)-

-->> How to successfully make a cup of tea...

  1. Get an empty cup...; make sure you wash/rinse it out very carefully; then, use a towel to dry the cup.
  2. Next, get a tea bag...and, place the tea bag into the bottom of the cup.
  3. Place some water into a kettle to boil...; wait until when the kettle is fully boiled.
  4. Next, pour the hot kettle of boiling water into the cup...until it almost reaches the very topmost brim.
  5. Get some sugar...and, dish out either one/or, two tablespoons full of sugar into the cup.
  6. As an alternative to sugar, one can also choose to sweeten tea with a spoonful of honey, instead.
  7. You might if you wish add some milk...either dairy milk/or, soya bean/some old fashioned people tend to use a tin of Carnation milk/-etc.
  8. Finally, be sure to stir all of the contents together...before you do sip the 'piping hot' drink down.
